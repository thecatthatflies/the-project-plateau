About Me

Hello! My name is Aiyan Chowdhury, and I'm a 6th-grade student who enjoys fishing (though I am DEFINITELY not great at it) and coding. I built this site to share my studies and ideas in a fun and interactive way, hoping it will inspire others to learn and explore with me.

I was lucky to be part of the Gifted and Talented research program, which gave me an opportunity to investigate a subject that I often ponder: homelessness. In this paper, I share some primary research I was able to do by interviewing several experts in the field. It is through this that a deep understanding of homelessness was gathered and helped even more to believe in knowledge to bring about change or meaningful conversations.

Hopefully, this website will provide an avenue for sharing my learning process and maybe even stir the curiosity of others!
