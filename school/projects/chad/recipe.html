Chadian Recipe

Traditional Recipe: Daraba

Daraba is a Chadian vegetable stew made with okra, sweet potatoes, eggplant, and peanut butter. It's flavorful, filling, and represents local cooking traditions.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 sweet potato, diced
  • 1 cup okra, sliced
  • 1 cup spinach or leafy greens
  • 1 cup eggplant, diced
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 4 cups vegetable broth or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Saut√© onions and garlic in oil until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until they break down.
  3. Mix in vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir peanut butter with broth and add to the pot.
  5. Simmer for 20 minutes, then add greens and seasonings.
